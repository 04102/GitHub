TraqDash 2.80 Release Notes

MUST BE USED WITH SU 3.70 OR LATER

Enhancements
------------
Added capability for WiFi downloads (when used with TraqDash WiFi SD card.

Enhanced screen backgrounds

Implemented camera icon for TraqDash USB cameras

Streamline screen operation for faster response

Bug Fixes
---------

Fixed sync problem with cameras on AutoX and Drag modes

Improved sync issue between SU and TraqDash during temporary power glitches
